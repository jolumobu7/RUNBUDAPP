Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Run Bud?

Simply download the app, create your account, and start tracking your first run! The app will guide you through the initial setup and help you set your running goals.

What features does Run Bud offer?

Run Bud provides GPS tracking, pace monitoring, distance logging, personalized running plans, progress analytics, achievement badges, and social features to connect with other runners.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. We take your privacy seriously. Your running data is encrypted and stored securely. You control what information you share with other users.

Can I sync Run Bud with other fitness apps?

Yes! Run Bud integrates with popular fitness platforms and health apps. Check the app settings for available integrations.

Does Run Bud work offline?

Yes, you can track your runs without an internet connection. Your data will sync automatically when you're back online.

How do I report a bug or technical issue?

Please email us at support@runbudapp.com with details about the issue, including your device model and app version. Screenshots are helpful too!

Is there a subscription fee?

Run Bud offers both free and premium tiers. The free version includes core tracking features, while premium unlocks advanced analytics, custom training plans, and ad-free experience.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription through your device's app store settings (App Store for iOS, Google Play for Android). For assistance, contact us at support@runbudapp.com.
