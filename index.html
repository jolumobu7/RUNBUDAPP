Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with RunBud?

Simply download the app, create your account, and start tracking your first run! The app will guide you through the initial setup and help you set your running goals.

What features does RunBud offer?

RunBud provides GPS tracking, pace monitoring, distance logging, personalized running plans, progress analytics, achievement badges, and social features to connect with other runners.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. We take your privacy seriously. Your running data is encrypted and stored securely. You control what information you share with other users.

Can I sync RunBud with other fitness apps?

Yes! RunBud integrates with popular fitness platforms and health apps. Check the app settings for available integrations.

Does RunBud work offline?

Yes, you can track your runs without an internet connection. Your data will sync automatically when you're back online.

How do I report a bug or technical issue?

Please email us at jolumobuhelp@gmail.com with details about the issue, including your device model and app version. Screenshots are helpful too!

Is RunBud free to use?

Yes! RunBud is completely free to use with all features available at no cost. We're committed to making running tracking accessible to everyone.
